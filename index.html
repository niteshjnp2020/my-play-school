School Services

High Quality PlaySchool

Pink and Blue playschool will provide a stimulating and safe environment for children aged 18 months to 4 years. Founded and led by Bhawna Bansal, an experienced former Pre-school educator with an MBA in HR and over 15 years of overall experience in the field, Pink and Blue is driven by a passion for excellence in early childhood education.
Innovative Teaching:- Our teaching methodology is tailored to engage young minds, fostering creativity and critical thinking skills from an early age.

Focused Sensory Learning:-

We believe in the power of sensory experiences to enhance learning. Through hands-on activities and exploration, children engage their senses to make meaningful connections with the world around them.
